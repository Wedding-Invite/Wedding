Our Love Story

A short timeline of our love story from the day we met.

19
Jan

The first met

I want you to know that I fell in love with you when we first met, you got my heart already. Would you believe that we met for such a short time and barely got a word but still loved? I never would have thought that the day we met each other was the best day of my entire life. You are really the one for me and I cannot help but wonder if it is because of that first time. You made a first impression on me that I can’t forget, and it must have made me fall for you. No one else in the world can make me forget that you are the one who loves me so much. You made me believe in love, you made me want to trust again right at that very special day. Let me be the one to tell you that I love you because I couldn’t say it on that first day we met.

14
Feb

The Proposal

Love is a beautiful feeling that has made me crazy. Yes, I am crazy about you. I am so lucky that I am in love with this much loving person. You are the one who has made my life complete and now I don’t need anything more. I just want you to be with me till the end. I love you and want you to love me too.

26
Feb

Engagement

Anyone can catch your eye, but it takes someone special to catch your heart. Our parents decided to confirm our wedding and we invite our close relatives for the occasion of our engagement. We exchanged the ring and confirmed that we are going to marry soon. This day is our big day in life where we began the exact love story.

29
Aug

Our wedding

A good wedding is one which allows for change and growth in the individuals and in the way they express their love. Whatever our souls are made of, we are the same. My love for you is a journey, starting at forever and ending at never.
